Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computer systems from cyber attacks.

Its main focus for protection is referred to as the CIA triad.

Image from vecteezy.com

Confidentiality: Ensuring private information is only accessible to authorized users.

Integrity: Ensuring accuracy and trustworthiness of data.

Availability: Systems must be accessible when needed by authorized users.


Some Areas of Cybersecurity

Network Security: Protecting networks from unauthorized access.

Application Security: Protecting software applications with secure coding and threat testing.

Information Security: Ensuring privacy and protection of stored and transmitted data.

Cloud Security: Protection of data hosted on cloud platforms.
