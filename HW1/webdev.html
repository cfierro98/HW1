Web Development is the creation, building, and maintaining of websites and applications.

It can be classified into two main categories: Front-End and Back-End

Image from vecteezy.com

Front-End Development

Front-End development, or client-side development, focuses on user interface and the visual aspects of a website or application.

Front-End developers use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to design the layout and interactive elements of their websites.


Back-End Development

Back-End development, or server-side development, deals with servers, databases, and application logic.

Back-End developers focus on what happens behind the scenes, rather than what the user sees.

They use programs such as Python, Java, and Node.js to handle all server-side operations.


Honorable Mention: Full-Stack Development

Full-Stack development involves working on both the front-end and back-end of applications.

Full-Stack developers have the ability to create entire websites by handling servers and databases and also the design aspect of their applications.
